First, choose a difficulty level on right side, and then click start game, during the game you can click start game to control the bird. Remember to avoid pigs and dropping to the floor. Hitting pipes will reduce one life, and you only have 3 lives. Passing a pipe will add 2 scores and hitting a white egg can add 5 scores. However if the pig hits the white egg before bird, 2 scores will be took off. Hitting a gold egg will give you 6 seconds snooze time.
